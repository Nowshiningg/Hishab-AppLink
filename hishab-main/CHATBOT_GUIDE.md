### Step 1: Setup Monthly Income (Required)
The chatbot needs your monthly income to provide budget advice.

1. Open the app on your phone
2. Go to **Settings** tab (bottom right)
3. Tap on **"Monthly Income"** field
4. Enter your monthly income amount (e.g., 50000)
5. Tap **Save**

### Step 2: Add Some Expenses
The chatbot analyzes your expense data. Add at least 2-3 expenses to see meaningful results.

1. Go to **Home** tab
2. Tap the **"+ Add Expense"** button (orange FAB)
3. Enter:
   - Amount: (e.g., 500)
   - Category: Select a category (e.g., Food, Transport)
   - Date: Today or past date
   - Description: (optional)
4. Tap **Save**
5. Repeat 2-3 times with different amounts and categories

**Example Expenses:**
- 500 BDT - Food
- 200 BDT - Transport
- 300 BDT - Shopping

### Step 3: Launch the Chatbot
1. Go to **Home** tab
2. Look for the **"Finbro Assistant"** or **"üí¨ Chatbot"** button in the quick actions (right side)
3. Tap it to open the chatbot

**English Questions:**
- "What was my biggest expense?"
- "How much did I spend this month?"
- "How much can I spend today?"
- "Am I spending more than last month?"
- "What's my daily budget?"

## üéØ Chatbot Features

### What Finbro Can Do:
1. **Biggest Expense** - Shows your largest single expense
2. **Monthly Spending** - Total spent this month
3. **Budget Check** - Tell if you can afford a specific amount
4. **Spending Trends** - Compare this month vs last month
5. **Category Breakdown** - Spending by category
6. **Today's Spending** - Total spent today
7. **Daily Budget** - How much you can spend per day

### How It Works:
- You can type questions or use the **microphone icon** for voice input
- The bot analyzes your expenses and income to provide answers
- Responses are in your selected language (English/Bengali)
- Processing indicator shows while the bot thinks

## üé§ Voice Input Tips

1. Tap the **microphone icon** in the input field
2. Start speaking clearly
3. The text will auto-populate as you speak
4. The mic icon highlights when listening
5. Tap it again to stop listening, then send your message

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Chatbot Button Not Showing?
- Make sure you're on the **Home** tab
- The buttons are below the daily spending section
- Look for "Finbro Assistant" / "‡¶´‡¶ø‡¶®‡¶¨‡ßç‡¶∞‡ßã ‡¶∏‡¶π‡¶æ‡¶Ø‡¶º‡¶ï" text

### "I didn't understand" Response?
- Make sure you've added income and expenses first
- Try asking simpler questions like "How much did I spend?"
- Check that your question matches the supported topics above

### Voice Input Not Working?
- Grant microphone permission to the app
- Make sure you're in a quiet environment
- Try using text input instead

### Getting Default Response?
- The bot responds with a default message if it doesn't understand
- Try asking the example questions first
- Make sure your question includes keywords like:
  - biggest, largest, most expensive
  - this month, monthly, total
  - today, spending
  - budget, afford, daily
  - category name (food, transport, shopping, etc.)
